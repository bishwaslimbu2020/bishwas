about me

My name is Bishwas Limbu. I was born on 18th October 1987. I am originally from Nepal but I have been residing in Cwmbran, South Wales since 2008.

I graduated from the University of South Wales with a bachelor's and master's degree in Animation (Hons). Despite this, I have never applied for a position in the Animation industry. Thanks to my friend who exposed me to the world of Web development.

I started coding late, but sometimes it's better to start late than never. One day, I observed my friend coding on his laptop which piqued my interest in coding. Ever since, I have not looked back. HTML, CSS and Javascript are my bread and butter. During my internship, though, I worked on a project using Bootstrap. I also have a fantastic eye for design. Recently, I've been gravitating toward React JS. In the next two years, I would love to see myself as a full stack developer or MERN developer.
